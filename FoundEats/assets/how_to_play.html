How to Play


BASICS

Select 'MealPlan' to create a a meal plan for the week. From there, select the day from the list, then whether you're planning breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

This will bring up the "Make a Meal" screen. From here, Select which foods you want for this meal, and observe how calories, fats, carbs, and protein values increase for that meal. Repeat this process for every day of your meal plan. The meals you create for the next seven days will be used to create a grocery list, populated by the foods selected in your meal plan. Then, we you acquire and check off the items for each meal in the real world, you complete a planned meal!

ETC.
As you complete certain achievements, the app will log when and where you accomplished these achievements on the 'Achievements' screen. You can view these on the 'Map' screen as well, which will also show you where you can get foods to fill out your planned meals.
